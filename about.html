About site

Purpose of the site

The idea to create this site came on a cozy autumn evening. I cooked cocoa, took a blanket and began to look for a series for viewing. Unfortunately, the popular trackers of serials had many shortcomings, from the copied description of the series and ending with a lot of obtrusive advertising. I wanted to create something of my own. What will be really convenient and practical. On the site you can not just choose a good series, but also read the author's materials about each of the series available on the site. So, enjoy)
